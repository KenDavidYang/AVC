About Us

Established in February of 2001, under the name of CFS Printing Inc. it is basically organized to cater to all the printing requirements of the Sharp group of companies and its affiliates. After a year of acting practically as a support group, the management showed satisfaction with its performance in handling pressured projects and the team's ability to deliver on the dot.

Basing primarily on the group's effectiveness and efficiency, the CF Sharp management realized the team's potential to go out of its own, as an acknowledged entity, and be market competitive in the printing industry where it practically belongs.
